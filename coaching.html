1-on-1 Coaching

Audience & Purpose

This page is designed for individuals who want structured, personalized fitness coaching. Its purpose is to explain the 1-on-1 Echelon Fits coaching offer, highlight the benefits, and give clear next steps for getting started.

What You Get with Echelon Fits Coaching

Echelon Fits coaching is built for people who are ready to train with intention instead of guessing. Each plan is created around your schedule, goals, and current level so you can make steady, measurable progress.

  • Customized weekly training sessions tailored to your goals and equipment.
  • Structured programming that balances strength, performance, and recovery.
  • Technique feedback through form checks to keep you safe and efficient.
  • Simple nutrition guidance that supports your training without being extreme.
  • Consistent accountability through check-ins and adjustments as you improve.

Who This Coaching Is For

This 1-on-1 coaching is a strong fit if you:

  • Want a clear plan instead of walking into the gym guessing what to do.
  • Balance training with school, work, sports, or a busy schedule.
  • Care about doing exercises with correct form and building confidence under the bar.
  • Are ready to show up, stay consistent, and be held accountable.

Next Steps: How to Get Started

  1. Fill out a brief intake form so coaching can be tailored to your goals and schedule.
  2. Schedule a short intro call to confirm expectations and ask questions.
  3. Receive your personalized program and walkthrough within 48 hours.
  4. Begin training with weekly check-ins and ongoing adjustments as you progress.
